February 10

Assorted notes and news

In lab meeting, we discussed a manuscript that I can't say much about. It's an interesting paper that I hope I will be able to share soon.

My submission to Behavior Research Methods -- [17] -- was rejected over the weekend. Bummer. I'll be spending some time over the next few weeks figuring out what the right path to take is to get the information contained within into the literature. I have lots of other projects to focus on so whatever solution I come up with shouldn't be too complicated.

Subject running continues to go smoothly, with 45 of 64 subjects completed. Unfortunately, only nine are signed up for this week (Tuesday through Friday), so I hope to add on another 10 either through additional signups or the week after this one.

In other news, I signed up for the Go! St. Louis marathon, so my time over the next two months will be spent sitting at my desk and running like a madman around St. Louis City.
