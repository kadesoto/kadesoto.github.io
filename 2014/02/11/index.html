Support Jason and Farah's research efforts!

My colleagues Jason Finley and Farah Naaz, Ph.D. memory researchers at Washington University, announced yesterday that they're starting a crowdfunding effort to support a research project titled, "How Does Technology Affect Our Memory?"

Over the next month they're hoping to collect money to run participants on Amazon Mechanical Turk and need support from backers who love behavioral science.

Here's the link if you want to contribute something!

Backing the project allows you access to their private research notes, and if you contribute enough, they'll throw in a signed copy of the resulting research or even thank you in the Acknowledgments section of the paper. Of course, the real reason to kick in is to support scientific progress.
