Reviewed a paper and a science fair project

Spent some time last Friday and this morning reviewing a submission to Psychology & Neuroscience, which I believe is primarily a Brazilian journal (thanks for finding me, guys). I'll bring it up again if I see it in print over the next few months. It's nice seeing the degree to which findings in the U.S. hold up in other countries.

Also, I spent a few minutes looking over an empirical project on memory an acquaintance of mine is working on for his ninth grade science fair project. It is a neat project and I'm glad to have been involved (to a small degree) during the research process. Perhaps I'll be able to talk about the project in more detail at a later date, too.
