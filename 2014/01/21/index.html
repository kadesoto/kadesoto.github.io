Working on Festschrift chapter

Some of the helpful comments Roddy and I got back on a chapter draft are concerned with the degree to which outliers may be driving correlational reports published in Roediger and DeSoto (2014) and DeSoto and Roediger (2014) .

Here's the text of the comment: "What is the correlation if one discards all points with X and Y that are say 2 (95% confidence interval) or 2.58 (99% confidence interval) beyond their means. (Perhaps this was done in the original article. If not, doing it here would be a good extension of the earlier findings.)"

When I get in tomorrow, I'll have to go through these issues. I'm almost entirely confident the results will still hold up (given that confidence is my line of work, I can't in good faith report 100% confidence), but we'll let the numbers speak for themselves. I'll post the results of those analyses when I'm able to get to them.
