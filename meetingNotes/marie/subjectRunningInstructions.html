Subject Running Instructions

Before the experiment:

  • Find out who's coming at http://experimetrix.com/wu. Go to "Experimenter Area." Logon is 1060; I will provide the password to you separately. Go to "View Schedule" and go to "Future: Taken." It often helps to print this page.

  • Make sure you have enough information sheets, debriefing sheets, and green payment vouchers. Information sheets and debriefing sheets can be printed out from their respective links. Green payment vouchers can be obtained from Andy or other lab members.

  • Andy should have already reserved your testing room. Go that room (the default is 337A) and click on the bottom right icon on the Windows desktop. It should read "DeSoto Experiment 15-03." Enter in the appropriate subject number, making sure to not use a previous one, and click submit. Maximize the screen. The computer is now ready for a subject.

  • If you cannot log on to the Windows partition of the computer, please consult a lab member or call or text me.

During the experiment:

  • Be in the main lab area when you expect subjects to arrive. Please err on the side of being early and not late. When subjects arrive, confirm that they are present for an experiment and ask their last name or initials, which you will cross-check with the schedule you saw or printed out earlier. Give them the information sheet and tell them to read it over and ask if there are any questions. If you are waiting for more subjects, ask the subject to wait in a chair. Use your judgment in determining how long to wait, up to 10 minutes past the scheduled start time.

  • Bring all the subjects to the testing room and ask them to sit at the prepared computers. Tell them that the experiment will last 60 minutes and all the instructions will be presented on the computer screen. Mention that there will be a part in the experiment in which subjects will be asked to indicate that they have finished reading some instructions, and that you will wait in the room until that point.

  • When you reach that point in the experiment -- approximately 10 minutes into the study -- and everyone has caught up, please read the additional instruction sheet and ask subjects if they have any questions. Answer them and then leave the room.

After the experiment:

  • When subjects come to you to tell you they are completed, give them the debriefing sheet and a green payment voucher, signed by a lab member, or note to yourself that they have earned credit. Answer any questions they might have.

  • E-mail Andy the subject's name (for now) so that he can assign the credit.

These instructions can be found online at: http://technimentis.com/meetingNotes/marie/subjectRunningInstructions.html
